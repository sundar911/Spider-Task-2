So here's how you play-

  • Once you press start bubbles will slowly be generated across your screen
  • Simply click on a bubble to pop it
  • Bubbles will fill your screen up faster as time progresses so keep those fingers ready!
  • When more than three quarters of your screen fills up for more than 10 seconds you lose :/
  • Keep popping bubbles to get points, every bubble carries one point
  • Lightning can be used thrice to eliminate half the bubbles on screen each time it's pressed , but if you do press it more than thrice bubbles will be generated faster with every illegal press
